Space Timeline

Key moments in rocket history

1957

Sputnik Launch 🛰️

The Soviet Union launches Sputnik 1, the first artificial satellite, aboard an R-7 rocket. The space age begins.

1961

First Human in Space 👨‍🚀

Yuri Gagarin becomes the first human in space aboard Vostok 1, completing one orbit of Earth.

1969

Moon Landing 🌙

Apollo 11's Saturn V rocket carries Neil Armstrong and Buzz Aldrin to the Moon. "One small step for man..."

1981

Space Shuttle Era 🛸

NASA's Space Shuttle Columbia makes its first orbital flight, beginning the era of reusable spacecraft.

2015

First Rocket Landing 🎯

SpaceX successfully lands a Falcon 9 first stage, proving orbital-class rocket reusability is possible.

2024

Starship Catches Booster 🏗️

SpaceX catches a Super Heavy booster with the launch tower's "chopstick" arms — a first in rocket history.
